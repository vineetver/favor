## What's New

- **Points don't overlap anymore** - Smart spacing keeps points visible while grouping by category
- **Click any point** - Instantly jump to that row in the table below the chart
- **Filter by category** - Click category buttons to show/hide different trait types
- **Works on mobile** - Chart adjusts to smaller screens

## How It Helps

- **See patterns faster** - Visual clusters show which traits are related
- **Find important results** - Triangle shapes mark significant findings
- **Navigate easily** - Click chart points to see details in the table
- **Share findings** - Charts make it easier to discuss results with colleagues

The old way required scrolling through tables to find patterns. Now you can see everything at once and click to explore details.
