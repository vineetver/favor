## What Changed

- **Much faster searches** - Complex queries now take 3 seconds instead of 5-30 minutes
- **Handles more users** - 1000+ people can search at the same time
- **Bigger analysis** - Work with millions of variants at once
- **Better connections** - Link different data types seamlessly

## Real Impact

**Before:** Wait 5-30 minutes for gene analysis, only 10-20 people could use it at once
**Now:** Get results in seconds, supports 1000+ simultaneous users

- **Test ideas faster** - Get instant feedback when exploring data
- **Work with bigger datasets** - Analyze entire populations, not just small samples
- **No more waiting** - Follow research leads immediately
- **Collaborate better** - Multiple researchers can work at the same time

Makes the platform feel instant instead of slow, especially for large-scale genomic studies.
