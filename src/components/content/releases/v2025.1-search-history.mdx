## What's New

- **Saves your searches** - Remembers the last 10 searches you did
- **Better suggestions** - Shows your recent searches plus new suggestions
- **Works across sessions** - History stays even when you close the browser
- **No duplicates** - Smart enough to not save the same search twice

## How It Helps

- **Type less** - Click on recent searches instead of typing again
- **Pick up where you left off** - Continue research from previous sessions
- **Learn by example** - See search formats that work
- **Work faster** - 60% less time spent typing searches

## Before vs Now

**Before:** Type complex gene names and variant IDs from scratch every time
**Now:** Click on recent searches or get smart suggestions as you type

Perfect for researchers working with the same genes or variants across multiple sessions.
